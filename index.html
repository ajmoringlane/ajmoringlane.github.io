Team Hi-Fi Prototype: [Official Demo]

Using Mockitt, we have made are first high fidelity prototype of our app. This demo highlights our login & signup features, where you can create and use your account to join the community. Once logged in, you have the option of adding or browsing tips. When you want to search for a tip, you will first choose the character you are looking for help on. After that, you will be taken to a catalog of information for the choosen character. These tips are all posted by other member of the community. When you want to share a tip, you would choose your character, and then type out the advice you would want to share. After clicking the arrow, the tip would be posted for other members to observe and learn from!
